## Getting started

### Create Account

You can sign up to Upstash using your Amazon, Github or Google accounts.
Alternatively you can sign up using email/password registration if you don't
want to use these auth providers, or you want to sign up using a corporate email
address.

<Info>
We do not access your information other than:

- Your email
- Your name
- Your profile picture and we never share your information with third parties.
</Info>
